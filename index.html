Tribute Page dedicated to Carmen Miranda

Carmen Miranda (February 9, 1909 – August 5, 1955), was a Portuguese-born Brazilian samba singer, dancer, Broadway actress, and film star who was popular from the 1930s to the 1950s. Carmen Miranda is an icon and as such she deserves a nice tribute. I made this page using HTML and CSS and to make it more interactive I used some animations on the images.
